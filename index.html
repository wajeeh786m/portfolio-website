About

Still early in the path —
learning it deliberately.

I'm a student with a strong interest in Computer Science, currently working through the fundamentals — programming, data structures, and how systems fit together — by building things rather than just reading about them.

I don't have years of professional experience yet, and I'd rather say that plainly than dress it up. What I do have is consistency: I show up, break things, fix them, and keep a record of what I learn along the way. Most of what's in this portfolio comes from small, self-directed projects built to understand a concept properly, not to pad a list.

I'm especially drawn to software development, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity, and I'm slowly getting more comfortable with web technologies and open source as I read other people's code and start contributing where I can. Long term, I want to work on software engineering problems that actually matter to the people using them.
